Mind & Actions Inc.
Minds & Actions is a nonprofit advancing inclusive learning, participatory research, and community-centered technology design across the lifespan. We bring children, people with disabilities, older adults, families, and communities together to learn, create, connect, and co-design meaningful action.
Current activies:
Together Lab — youth inclusion, social activities, collaborative learning, peer connection
AgeWell Lab — older adults, digital inclusion, healthy aging, community participation
Community Lab — public education, exhibits, workshops, awareness-to-understanding events

Future activities:
Access Lab — disability inclusion, accessibility, assistive technology, independent living, inclusive design
Co-Design Lab — participatory research with autistic people, people with disabilities, older adults, and families
